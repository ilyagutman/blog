Borsch with Pork Ribs

Posted on 2018, May 01 by Ilya Gutman 2 mins read

INGREDIENTS

Ingredient Amount
beets 3 heads
carrots 2-3 pieces
cabbage ~ 1⁄2 head
crashed tomatoes 1 14 oz jar
onions 2 heads
pickling spice 1⁄2 tsp
pork ribs 1⁄2 rack
pork hocks 1-2 pieces
borsch spices mix 1 packet
salt 1⁄2 tsp
parsley 1 bunch
dill 1 bunch
basil leaves 2-3 pieces
garlic 5-6 cloves
black pepper ~ 10
vietnamese chili 1 tsp
distilled water ~ 2 gallons
(potatoes) 2-3 pieces

INSRUCTIONS

  1. Half a rack of pork ribs clean, cut and start boiling in the cold water, for about 2 hours. With it, add smoked pork hocks, beads of black pepper, 1 onion, 1 carrot. Remove foam every 20-30 minutes. Maintain broth clear, without foam and residue.

  2. At the same time, preparing “zapravka” from the onions, carrots and beets. First, fry the onions to transparency. Add beets and carrots, grinded with a coarse-grained grinder, and saute on a low heat. Add crashed tomatoes, Russian borsch spices mix, 1 tea spoon of the vietnamese chili paste. Sometimes I add chilli and/or sweet pepper. Saute for about 15-20 minutes, until solft.

  3. When broth is ready, remove meat, free off bones. Throw away boiled hocks, onion and carrot. If you want to have borsch with potatoes, now is the time to add them. Cut them in 1” pieces, otherwise they will completely disolve.

  4. When potatoes are half-ready, add vegetable “zapravka”, bring to boil and let it “bubble” for another 5 minutes.

  5. Shred fresh cabbage in thin slices and add to the pot. Taste, add salt and pickling spice.

  6. How long to boil the cabbage is a matter of taste. I boil for 6-7 minutes, so it still has a crunch to it.

  7. Add dill, parsley, minced garlic, basil leaves.

  8. Bring to boil and turn off the heat. Wrap the lid with a thin towel and cover the pot for a few hours.

  9. Done. Serve with sour cream.
