Crock-Pot Beef Roast

Posted on 2018, Apr 29 by Ilya Gutman One min read

INGREDIENTS

Ingredient Amount Instructions
potato 6 peeled and cut into large chunks
carrots 5 peeled and cut into 2-inch slices
onion 1 quartered
roast 2-3 lbs boneless chuck roast
broth 1⁄2 cup beef or chicken broth
chives 1 table spoon chopped
parsley 2 table spoons chopped
pepper 1⁄8 tea spoon
soup mix 1 oz package onion soup mix
sause 2 tbs Worcestershire sauce
soup cream 10 oz can cream of mushroom soup

INSRUCTIONS

  • Put potatoes, carrots and onion at the bottom of crockpot.
  • Place the roast on top (fat side up).
  • Pour in beef or chicken broth.
  • Sprinkle chives, parsley, pepper and Onion Soup mix over the meat.
  • Pour Worcestershire sauce and mushroom soup over meat.
  • Cover and cook on low 8-10 hours.
